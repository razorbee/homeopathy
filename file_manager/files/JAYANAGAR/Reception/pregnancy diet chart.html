PREGNANCY DIET CHART

Must Have:-

  1. 6- Almonds (Soaked & Pealed Skin)

  2. 6 Soft Dates (Dry 12- Daily) & 1 Table Spoon Honey

  3. Walnuts- 2

  4. Milk- 1/2 Leter/Day

  5. Fruits-2/Day

  6. 1 Boiled Egg

  7. Beat Root, Carrot, Tomato, Cucumber, Red Capsicum, Black Grapes.

  8. Urat dal- 1 Small Laddu Daily, Green Gram

  9. Brown Rice, Chapathi, Raagi

10) In Saffron- from 7th Month

11) Coconut Water

Avoid:-

  1. Papaya

  2. Pine Apple

  3. Seisme Seeds

  4. Mullangi (Raddish)

  5. Coffee/Tea.

NOTE:-

  1. DAILY 1/2 TO 1 HOUR WALKING

  2. WITH ROUTINE CARBOHYDRATED FOOD
